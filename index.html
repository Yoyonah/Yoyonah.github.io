About Me

I am a Game Design Student, studying Creative media and Game Technologies at Saxion University of Applied Sciences.
A little bit about me, I was born and raised on Curacao and always had a great passion for videogames and media in general.
My favorite type of games are puzzle games because there are always very creative and hard challenges to face and story adventure games because its great to see the different ways a story can be told or interpreted.
